MASTER SERVICES AGREEMENT

This Master Services Agreement is entered into between Northwind Analytics LLC and Acme Retail Group.
Effective Date: March 1, 2026.
Payment Terms: Net 30 from receipt of invoice.
Term: 12 months. The agreement renews automatically for one additional year unless either party gives 30 days written notice.

Data Privacy: Vendor will protect customer data and maintain reasonable technical safeguards.
Termination: Either party may terminate for material breach after 15 days notice and opportunity to cure.
Governing Law: State of New York.
Signature: Signed by authorized representatives.
